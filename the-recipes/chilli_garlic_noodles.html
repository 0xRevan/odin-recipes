Chilli Garlic Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 red chillies, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Spring onions, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sliced chillies. Saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar. Mix well.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring continuously.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onions if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your GarlicChilli Noodles!
