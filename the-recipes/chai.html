Chai

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp black tea leaves or 2 tea bags
  • 4-5 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add the crushed cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, cloves, and ginger slices.
  3. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the black tea leaves or tea bags and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the milk and bring to a boil again.
  6. Add sugar to taste and stir well.
  7. Strain the chai into cups and serve hot.
  8. Enjoy your delicious Chai!
