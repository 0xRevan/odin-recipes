Popcoin

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Butter, cheese powder, or seasoning of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the corn kernels and cover the pot with a lid.
  3. Shake the pot occasionally to ensure even popping.
  4. Once the popping slows down, remove the pot from heat.
  5. Season with salt and any other desired toppings.
  6. Enjoy your Popcoin!
